Grace Hopper

Queen of Software and U.S. Navy Admiral

A ship in port is safe, but this not what ships are built for. Sail out to sea and do new things.

Quick Facts

  • Birth: December 9, 1906
  • Birth Place: New York, USA
  • Alma Mater: Bachelors, Vassar College. Masters, Yale University.
  • Random Facts: She married Vincent Hopper but divorced in 1945... but she kept his surname. She needed an exemption to enlist in the Navy because she was 15lbs too light. She remained on active duty 7 years past mandatory retirement.
  • Contribution: One of the first programmers on Mark 1 computer and coauthored papers known as Automatic Sequence Controlled Calculator. Developed validation software for COBOL and its compiler. (She wanted programs to be written more like English than machine code!) Implemented standard for programming languages like FORTRAN and COBOL.
