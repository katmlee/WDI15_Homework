Statistics on Women in Computer Science

  • In the US, women earn 57% of all undergradute degrees, yet only 18% of undergraduate degrees are in computer and information science degrees.

  • In 1984, 37.1% of Computer Science degrees were awarded to women; the percentage dropped to 29.9% in 1989-1990, and 26.7% in 1997-1998.

  • A National Public Radio report in 2013 stated that about 20% of all U.S. computer programmers are female.
